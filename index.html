About

SNip Writings is your personal space to share real writing about stories, politics, food, and books. It is built to keep your voice easy to publish and simple for readers to browse.

Use the New Post tab to write directly in the browser. Your posts are saved locally in your browser, so you can keep writing and publish from here.

This site is meant to be a living journal that grows with your writing. Add a post, tag it with the right category, and then use the Posts tab to read and review your work.
